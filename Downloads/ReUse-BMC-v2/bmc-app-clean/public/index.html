How It Works

Every donation follows a transparent, tracked process

1

Donor Lists

Donor uploads item with photos & GPS location

2

BMC Assigns

BMC officer assigned to collect the item

3

Item Picked

Officer picks up & marks as collected

4

NGO Requests

Verified NGOs request the item

5

BMC Assigns

BMC picks best NGO by priority score

6

Submitted

BMC submits item; NGO marks received

7

Distributed

NGO distributes with photo evidence

8

Recycled

Leftover recycled; money for education & poor

🌐 Full Public Transparency

Every donation, distribution, and recycled rupee is visible to everyone. No hidden data.
